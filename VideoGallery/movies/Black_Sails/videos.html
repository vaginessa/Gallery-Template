Black Sails (2014-2017)
Description: The series centers on the tales of Captain Flint, who has a reputation throughout the West Indies as being the most brilliant, most feared of all the Golden Age pirates. The series is a prequel to Robert Louis Stevenson's "Treasure Island," and is set 20 years before the events depicted in that literary classic. Links: Season 1, Season 2, Season 3, Season 4.
