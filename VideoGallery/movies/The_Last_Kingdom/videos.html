The Last Kingdom (2015-?)
Description: Set in the late ninth century, when what is known as England today was several separate kingdoms.Young warrior and outsider, Uhtred, on a fierce mission to reclaim his birthright. Adapted from Bernard Cornwell’s best-selling series of books “The Saxon Stories,” The Last Kingdom combines real historical figures and events with fiction, re-telling the history of King Alfred the Great and his desire to unite the many separate kingdoms into what would become England.
